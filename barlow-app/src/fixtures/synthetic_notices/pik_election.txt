AGENT BANK NOTICE

Date:       January 10, 2025
Facility:   Delta Energy Holdings, Ltd. — First Lien Term Loan

RE: PIK INTEREST ELECTION — INTEREST PERIOD ENDING MARCH 31, 2025

To:   All Lenders of Record
From: Cascade Bank, as Administrative Agent

Pursuant to Section 4.02(b) of the Credit Agreement, dated as of September 30,
2021, among Delta Energy Holdings, Ltd., as Borrower, the lenders party thereto,
and Cascade Bank, as Administrative Agent, the Administrative Agent has received
proper notice of a PIK (pay-in-kind) interest election from Delta Energy Holdings,
Ltd., as follows:

  Loan ID:              L005
  PIK Interest Period:  January 1, 2025 through March 31, 2025
  Election Type:        Full PIK — interest to be capitalized, not paid in cash

Interest otherwise payable in cash during the PIK Interest Period shall be
capitalized and added to the outstanding principal balance of the Term Loan on
the last day of the PIK Interest Period (March 31, 2025), in accordance with
Section 4.02(b) of the Credit Agreement.

This election is permitted once per fiscal year under the terms of the Credit
Agreement. Cash interest payments shall resume for the interest period beginning
April 1, 2025, unless Delta Energy Holdings, Ltd. delivers a further PIK election
notice pursuant to Section 4.02(b).

Lenders should update their records to reflect this PIK election for Loan ID L005
for the period January 1, 2025 through March 31, 2025.

Cascade Bank
Administrative Agent
