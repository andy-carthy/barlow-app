AGENT BANK NOTICE

Date:       May 1, 2025
Reference:  Omnibus Amendment Notice — Multiple Credit Facilities

RE: AMENDMENT NOTICE — VARIOUS CREDIT AGREEMENTS

To:   All Lenders of Record
From: Regional Lending Partners, LLC, as Administrative Agent

This notice is to advise all lenders that certain amendments to the Credit
Agreements identified in Schedule 1 attached hereto have become effective as
of the Effective Date (as defined in each respective Amendment Agreement). A
copy of Schedule 1 will be distributed under separate cover.

The Applicable Rate for each affected Borrower shall be adjusted in accordance
with the Pricing Grid set forth in Exhibit A to the applicable Amendment
Agreement. Lenders should update their records accordingly upon receipt of the
final Exhibit A, which has not yet been finalized and will be circulated upon
completion.

Additionally, certain financial maintenance covenants have been modified as
described in the Amendment Agreements. The specific covenant changes are set
forth in Section 7.15 of each Amendment Agreement; those sections have not been
included in this notice transmission pending final execution of the Amendment
Agreements.

For facilities subject to a Ratings-Based Pricing Grid, the new applicable
spread will be determined upon receipt of updated agency ratings from S&P and
Moody's, which are currently under review and are not available as of the date
of this notice.

Lenders requiring clarification of the affected facilities, applicable effective
dates, or specific amended terms should contact the Agent's Transaction
Management desk at the number on file.

Regional Lending Partners, LLC
Administrative Agent
